Join The Room

Free video conferencing and screen sharing just by sharing url of the room.




Works in browsers supporting HTML5 AND WebRTC.

Video conferencing

Chrome (Desktop v23+, Android v28+)
Firefox (Desktop v22+, Android v24+)
Opera (Desktop v18+, Opera mobile v12+)
Chrome OS

Screen Sharing

Source (Chrome desktop v26+)
Viewers (A browser suporting video conferencing specified above)

FEATURES



Video

Everybody in the room can see everybody. Do video conferencing on the fly.

Share Desktop

Share desktop to show your screen to other people in the room. This works when using https:// url.

Easy

No signup. No hassle. No tracking.

Anonymous

Once you get into the room all traffic directly goes from peer to peer.
